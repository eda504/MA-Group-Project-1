Tips for Finding Your Pet

1. Report Immediately

If you lose your pet, report it to a shelter immediately, and be sure to leave your contact information along with a photo of your pet.

2. Flyers

When your pet is missing, put up flyers with descriptions/photos of your pet and your contact information in case someone spots your pet, so they can return it to you.

3. Calm Consistency

Keep trying to find your pet, and don't give up hope. Some pets are found days or weeks after reported missing.

4. Set Up Familiar Scents

Leave out their bed, litter box, or your worn clothing outside; the smell might make them come back.
